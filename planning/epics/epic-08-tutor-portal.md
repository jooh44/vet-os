# Epic 08: Tutor Portal

**Status:** Draft
**Priority:** Medium (MVP Requirement)
**Owner:** Product Owner

## Goal
Provide a dedicated access area for Tutors (clients) to view their pets' health data and manage appointments.

## Description
The Tutor Portal is a restricted area where clients can log in to view their pets' vaccination cards, history, and upcoming appointments. Even if simple for MVP, it's a key retention feature.

## Scope
### In Scope
- Tutor Login & Password Reset
- My Pets Dashboard
- Digital Vaccination Card View
- Appointment History View
- Request Cancellation/Reschedule

### Out of Scope
- Direct self-booking (Phase 2)
- Online Payments (Phase 2)
