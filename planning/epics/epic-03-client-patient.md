# Epic 03: Client & Patient Management

**Status:** Draft
**Priority:** High
**Owner:** Product Manager

## Goal
Enable the registration and management of Tutors (clients) and their Pets (patients), serving as the central database for clinical operations.

## Description
This epic covers the CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for Tutors and Pets. It establishes the "Digital Record" of the clinic's patients, which is prerequisite for scheduling and medical records.

## Scope
### In Scope
- Tutor registration and management (Search, Edit, View)
- Pet registration and management (Details, Photos, History View)
- Patient Timeline View (History of consults, vaccines, etc.)
- Basic search functionality

### Out of Scope
- Medical Record creation (belongs to Epic 05)
- Online booking by Tutor (Phase 2)
